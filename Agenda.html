AGENDA - VMware ESXi to OpenShift Virtualization Workshop

📊

Session focus areas:

  • Validate scope and inventory (clusters, hosts, VMs)
  • Align on migration strategy and guiding principles
  • Confirm application dependencies and topology
  • Define migration waves and prioritization approach
  • Agree on fallback, rollback, and stabilization strategy
  • Review infrastructure, capacity, and host re-use plan
  • Establish execution governance and rollout sequencing
Outcome: a shared migration plan, sequencing model, and risk-managed execution approach.
